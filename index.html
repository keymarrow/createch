Welcome to CREATECH

CREATECH is an Edu-tech organization dedicated to deliver tangible impact through technology on the basis of education by making students not only succeed academically but globally and using their classroom concepts to solve social problems. We also introduce youth to the Innovation ecosystem and engage, effectively in creating a more innovative generation, especially in Tech-based aspects. By offer STEM classes, school outreaches, after-school programs, science affair events and teachers training. We motivate, engage, and inspire the next generation of STEM leaders.
